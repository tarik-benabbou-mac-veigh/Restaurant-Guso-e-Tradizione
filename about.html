History of

Gusto e Tradizione

~~ How our project started ~~

Nestled in a charming lakeside town, Gusto e Tradizione was founded by renowned chefs Giovanni Moretti and Luca Bellucci, driven by their passion for authentic Italian cuisine.

Raised in Italy’s culinary heartlands, they envisioned a restaurant that honors traditional flavors while embracing modern techniques. Each dish tells a story—one of heritage, family recipes, and fresh, locally sourced ingredients.

~~ Our chefs ~~

The Culinary Soul of Gusto e Tradizione

Giovanni Moretti and Luca Bellucci form a passionate duo of chefs, blending tradition and creativity at Gusto e Tradizione.

Giovanni Moretti

Born in Tuscany, Giovanni Moretti grew up in a family where cooking was an art passed down through generations. After studying at the Culinary Institute of Florence, he refined his skills in renowned Italian trattorias.

A specialist in fresh pasta and slow-cooked sauces, Giovanni is dedicated to preserving ancestral recipes while enhancing each ingredient with precision and authenticity.

Luca Bellucci

Luca Bellucci, on the other hand, comes from Naples, the heart of Mediterranean cuisine. After training in modern gastronomy in Milan, he traveled across Italy and France to explore new culinary trends.

His style is distinguished by bold flavor combinations and a mastery of presentation. An expert in desserts and contemporary reinterpretations of Italian classics, Luca brings elegance and innovation to the restaurant’s dishes.
